Tableturf Bot Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2024-09-02

Data collection

Tableturf Bot collects basic user data from users that log into TableturfQ with their Discord account.
The access token and refresh token will never be shared, and can be revoked by users at any time by manually logging out from TableturfQ's profile page.
The bot does not collect data from Discord servers it is a member of, and the bot does not collect data from the messages within those servers.
