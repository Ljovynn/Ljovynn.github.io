Tableturf Bot Terms of Service

Last updated: 2024-09-02

Proper Usage

Tableturf Bot is a Discord Bot for various Tableturf Battle and TableturfQ utilities. Tableturf is a trademark of Nintendo and the bot is not affiliated with or sponsored by Nintendo.
Attempts to crash the bot, bypass safety features, overload bot usage, etc, should not be made.
The bot must not be used to generate profit in any way.
The bot may not be used to break Discord's Terms of Service.

Tableturf Bot Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2024-08-12

How Tableturf Bot collects data

Tableturf Bot collects basic user data from users that log into TableturfQ with Discord.
The access token and refresh token will never be shared, and can be revoked by users at any time by manually logging out from TableturfQ's profile page.
Tableturf Bot does not collect data from Discord servers it is a member of, and Tableturf Bot does not collect data from the messages within those servers.
